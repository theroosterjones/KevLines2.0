# Privacy Policy for KevLines

Effective date: March 26, 2026

KevLines ("the App") is developed by Kevin Jones. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

## Overview

KevLines analyzes exercise form from live camera input or videos you choose from your photo library. Processing is performed on-device.

## Data We Access

The App may request access to:

- Camera: to provide real-time exercise analysis.
- Photo Library: to let you select videos for analysis.

The App processes pose landmarks and angle calculations from video frames to generate analysis overlays and rep/tempo metrics.

## How Data Is Used

Data is used only to provide app functionality, including:

- Running pose and form analysis.
- Rendering overlays and metrics.
- Exporting analyzed videos you choose to save/share.

## Data Sharing and Selling

- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not share your videos or analysis data with third parties for advertising or marketing.
- The App does not include third-party analytics or ad SDKs.

## Data Storage and Retention

- Processing is performed locally on your device.
- Videos and outputs are handled through local iOS storage and system sharing flows.
- The current version does not require account creation.

You can remove app data by deleting exported files and/or uninstalling the App.

## Internet and Network Use

Core analysis features are designed to run without uploading your videos to a server.

## Your Choices

- You may deny camera or photo library access in iOS Settings at any time.
- You control what videos are selected and whether outputs are exported/shared.

## Children's Privacy

KevLines is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by an updated effective date.
